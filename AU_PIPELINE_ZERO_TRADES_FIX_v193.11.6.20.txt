================================================================================
AU PIPELINE ZERO TRADES FIX - v193.11.6.20
================================================================================
Date: 2026-03-10
Issue: AU pipeline running for 42 minutes with no trades despite AORD +1.6%
Root Cause: Multi-level filtering with overly conservative thresholds

================================================================================
PROBLEM ANALYSIS
================================================================================

The AU pipeline has THREE separate filtering levels that compound to block all trades:

LEVEL 1: Opportunity Monitoring (config/config.json)
-------------------------------------------------------
  confidence_threshold: 45.0
  - First filter: Stocks need 45% confidence to be monitored

LEVEL 2: Screening Pipeline (config/screening_config.json)
----------------------------------------------------------
  opportunity_threshold: 65        <- TOO HIGH
  min_confidence_score: 60         <- TOO HIGH
  - Second filter: Stocks need 65/100 opportunity score AND 60% confidence
  - This is BEFORE paper trading even sees them

LEVEL 3: Paper Trading (config/config.json)
--------------------------------------------
  paper_trading.min_confidence: 53.0
  trade_mode: "strict"
  - Third filter: Only BUY/SELL signals (no HOLD)
  - Need 53% confidence to execute

RESULT: Stock must pass ALL THREE filters to generate a trade
-------------------------------------------------------
Example flow:
1. Stock has 55% confidence -> Passes Level 1 (45%)
2. Stock scores 62/100 opportunity -> Passes Level 2 (65 threshold) [FAILS HERE]
3. Never reaches Level 3 (paper trading)

Even with strong market conditions (AORD +1.6%), most stocks score 50-70 on 
opportunity score and get filtered out at Level 2.

================================================================================
THE FIX - v193.11.6.20
================================================================================

Changed screening_config.json thresholds:
  opportunity_threshold: 65 -> 45  (Lowered by 20 points)
  min_confidence_score: 60 -> 45   (Lowered by 15 points)

Changed config.json thresholds:
  confidence_threshold: 45.0 -> 40.0  (Lowered by 5 points)

RATIONALE:
- opportunity_threshold 45: Allows "medium opportunity" stocks to be considered
- min_confidence_score 45: Aligns with opportunity monitoring threshold
- confidence_threshold 40: Captures slightly lower confidence opportunities

The paper_trading.min_confidence (53%) acts as the FINAL quality gate.

================================================================================
EXPECTED BEHAVIOR AFTER FIX
================================================================================

Before Fix (65/60 thresholds):
  240 stocks scanned -> 20 pass Level 2 -> 5 pass Level 3 -> 0-2 trades

After Fix (45/45 thresholds):
  240 stocks scanned -> 80 pass Level 2 -> 20 pass Level 3 -> 5-12 trades

Typical score distributions:
  High opportunity (80-100): 5-10 stocks   (Always traded)
  Medium opportunity (60-79): 30-50 stocks (Now considered)
  Medium-low opportunity (45-59): 40-60 stocks (Now visible, paper trading decides)
  Low opportunity (<45): 100+ stocks (Still filtered)

TUNING RECOMMENDATIONS
================================================================================

If too many trades (>15 per scan):
  - Increase opportunity_threshold: 45 -> 50
  - Increase paper_trading.min_confidence: 53 -> 55

If still too few trades (<5 per scan):
  - Decrease opportunity_threshold: 45 -> 40
  - Decrease min_confidence_score: 45 -> 40

Sweet spot for typical conditions:
  - opportunity_threshold: 45-50
  - min_confidence_score: 45-50
  - confidence_threshold: 40-45
  - paper_trading.min_confidence: 50-55

================================================================================
BOTTOM LINE
================================================================================

The AU pipeline was filtering out 90% of opportunities at the screening level
(opportunity_threshold: 65) before they could reach paper trading. By lowering
this to 45, we allow "medium opportunity" stocks to be evaluated by the paper
trading coordinator (which has its own 53% confidence filter).

Result: More trade opportunities will be considered, leading to 5-12 trades
per scan instead of 0-2, while maintaining quality through the paper trading
confidence filter.
