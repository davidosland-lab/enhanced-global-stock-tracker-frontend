=====================================
ZOOM FEATURES - QUICK REFERENCE GUIDE
=====================================

MOUSE CONTROLS:
- Scroll wheel: Zoom in/out
- Click & drag: Select area to zoom
- Ctrl + drag: Pan around chart
- Double-click: Quick zoom in

KEYBOARD SHORTCUTS:
- Plus (+): Zoom in
- Minus (-): Zoom out
- R key: Reset zoom
- ESC key: Reset zoom

TOUCH CONTROLS (Mobile):
- Pinch: Zoom in/out
- Two fingers: Pan chart
- Double tap: Zoom in
