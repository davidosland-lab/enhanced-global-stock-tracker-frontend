FEATURE 1: TRAIN INDIVIDUAL STOCKS
----------------------------------
Purpose: Train LSTM models for specific stocks (CBA.AX, NAB.AX, etc.)

How to Use:
1. START.bat → Option 8: "Train Individual Stocks"

2. Enter stock symbols:
   Stock #1: CBA.AX
   Stock #2: NAB.AX
   Stock #3: WBC.AX
   Stock #4: [Press Enter]

3. Confirm training:
   Proceed with training? (yes/no): yes

4. Wait ~3 min per stock

5. Restart dashboard to use new models

Benefits:
✓ No more -20% confidence penalty
✓ Full 5-component signals
✓ Fixes "LSTM failed - not enough data"


FEATURE 2: GAP REALITY CHECKER
------------------------------
Purpose: Validates gap predictions and adjusts stock scores

How it Works:
1. Before market open: Prediction stored
   state/gap_prediction_AU_20260310.json

2. 15 min after open: Validation runs automatically
   - Compares predicted vs actual gap
   - Calculates accuracy rating
   - Determines score multiplier (0.80x to 1.20x)

3. During trading: Scores auto-adjusted
   - Stronger than predicted → boost scores 5-20%
   - Weaker than predicted → reduce scores 5-20%

Manual Validation (optional):
```python
from core.paper_trading_coordinator import PaperTradingCoordinator
coord = PaperTradingCoordinator(['CBA.AX'])
results = coord.validate_gap_predictions()
print(results)
```
