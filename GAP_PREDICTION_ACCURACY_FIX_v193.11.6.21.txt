FIX #2: GAP REALITY CHECKER MODULE
-----------------------------------
File: core/gap_reality_checker.py (NEW FILE, 16.6 KB)

FUNCTIONALITY:
1. Store predictions (before market open)
   - Records predicted gap, confidence, direction
   - Saves to state/gap_prediction_{MARKET}_{DATE}.json

2. Validate predictions (10-15 min after market open)
   - Fetches actual market open price
   - Calculates actual gap vs yesterday's close
   - Computes prediction error (actual - predicted)

3. Calculate accuracy rating
   - EXCELLENT:    < 0.2% error → 1.00x multiplier
   - GOOD:         0.2-0.5% error → 1.05x or 0.95x
   - POOR:         0.5-1.0% error → 1.10x or 0.90x
   - TERRIBLE:     1.0-2.0% error → 1.15x or 0.85x
   - CATASTROPHIC: > 2.0% error → 1.20x or 0.80x

4. Apply score adjustments
   - Positive surprise (stronger than predicted) → BOOST scores
   - Negative surprise (weaker than predicted) → REDUCE scores
   - Affects all stocks from that market proportionally

EXAMPLE (Today's AU Market):
  Predicted:  +0.38%
  Actual:     +1.3%
  Error:      +0.92% (positive surprise - TERRIBLE accuracy)
  Rating:     TERRIBLE_POSITIVE
  Multiplier: 1.15x
  Effect:     All AU stock scores boosted 15%
              - Stock with 65.0 score → 74.75
              - Stock with 58.0 score → 66.70
              - Adjusts for stronger-than-expected market
