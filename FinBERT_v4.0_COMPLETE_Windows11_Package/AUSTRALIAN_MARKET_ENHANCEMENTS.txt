HOW IT WORKS
================================================================================

Stock Symbol Detection:
-----------------------
When you analyze an Australian stock (e.g., BHP.AX, CBA.AX, WBC.AX):

1. System detects ".AX" suffix
2. Automatically enables Australian data sources
3. Fetches from ALL 6 sources concurrently
4. Combines and analyzes all articles with FinBERT
5. Returns comprehensive sentiment analysis

Example: BHP.AX (BHP Group)
---------------------------
Standard sources (2):
  - Yahoo Finance: BHP company news
  - Finviz: International BHP news

Australian sources (4):
  - RBA: Interest rate impacts on mining sector
  - Treasury: Mining tax policies, export forecasts
  - ABS: Trade balance, commodity export data
  - AFR: BHP earnings, analyst coverage

Total sources: 6 (vs 2 for non-Australian stocks)
