================================================================================
HOW TO SEE SENTIMENT IN UI
================================================================================

1. Start application: START_FINBERT_V4.bat
2. Open browser: http://localhost:5000
3. Enter stock symbol: AAPL
4. Click "Analyze Stock"
5. Look for "Market Sentiment" section (right side)
6. Should show:
   - Sentiment badge (POSITIVE/NEUTRAL/NEGATIVE)
   - Confidence percentage
   - Score bars (positive/neutral/negative %)
   - Compound score (-1.0 to +1.0)
   - News article count
   - List of recent news headlines
