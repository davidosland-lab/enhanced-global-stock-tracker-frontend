GAP PREDICTION METHODOLOGY:
==========================

UK FTSE Gap Prediction:
-----------------------
Weighting:
- US Markets: 65% (S&P 50%, NASDAQ 30%, DOW 20%)
- Overnight Futures: 15% (Z=F FTSE futures)
- Europe: 10% (DAX, CAC correlation 0.40)
- FX & Commodities: 10% (GBP/USD 50%, Oil 25%, Metals 25%)

Confidence Adjustment:
- Base confidence: 75%
- VIX < 20: +5% (calm markets = higher confidence)
- VIX 20-30: 0% (normal volatility)
- VIX > 30: -10% (high volatility = lower confidence)

Example (2026-03-09 data):
- S&P 500: -1.33%, NASDAQ: -1.59%, DOW: -0.95%
- DAX: -0.94%, FTSE futures: unavailable
- Oil: +24.6% (Iran conflict)
- VIX: 29.49
Result: +0.50% bullish gap (driven by oil), 70% confidence

AU SPI Gap Prediction:
---------------------
Weighting:
- US Markets: 65% (S&P 50%, NASDAQ 30%, DOW 20%)
- Overnight Futures: 15% (ES, NQ S&P/NASDAQ futures)
- UK/Europe: 10% (FTSE, DAX)
- Commodities: 10% (AUD/USD 50%, Iron Ore 25%, Oil 25%)

Confidence Adjustment: Same as UK (VIX-based)

Example (2026-03-09 data):
Result: -0.57% bearish gap, 70% confidence

POSITION SIZING LOGIC:
=====================

Formula: base_mult × confidence_factor × gap_factor × risk_multiplier

Components:
-----------
1. Base Multiplier (0.5 - 1.0):
   - AU: 0.925x (correlation 0.85)
   - UK: 0.875x (correlation 0.75)

2. Confidence Factor (0.6 - 1.2):
   - 60% confidence = 0.6x
   - 70% confidence = 0.85x
   - 80% confidence = 1.0x
   - 90% confidence = 1.2x

3. Gap Factor (0.7 - 1.3):
   - 0.3% gap = 0.7x
   - 1.0% gap = 1.0x
   - 2.0% gap = 1.3x

4. Risk Multiplier (0.5 - 1.0):
   - World Risk < 80: 1.0x
   - World Risk 80-85: 0.75x
   - World Risk >= 85: 0.5x
   
5. Sentiment Adjustment (-15%):
   - If sentiment and gap disagree: × 0.85

Final Position: Clamped to 0.25x - 1.5x

EXAMPLES:
--------
UK Scenario (2026-03-09):
- Gap: +0.50%, Confidence: 70%, Sentiment: 29.2, World Risk: 87.6

- Decision: BLOCK (extreme bearish sentiment + high risk)

RISK GATES:
==========

Gate 1: Confidence Threshold
- Minimum: 60%
- Blocks trades with low prediction confidence

Gate 2: Gap Magnitude
- Minimum: 0.3%
- Ignores tiny gaps (noise)

Gate 3: World Risk Check
- >= 85: Position reduced 50%
- >= 80: Position reduced 25%

Gate 4: Sentiment Alignment
- Extreme bearish (< 20) + negative gap: BLOCK
- Bearish (< 35) + negative gap: Reduce position 25%

Gate 5: Direction Consistency
- Gap and sentiment disagree: Reduce position 15%

ENTRY TIMING:
============

Based on gap magnitude and confidence:

- NOW: Gap >= 1.5% AND Confidence >= 75%
  → Strong conviction, enter immediately

- WAIT_2MIN: Gap >= 1.0% AND Confidence >= 70%
  → Good signal, wait for initial volatility

- WAIT_5MIN: Gap >= 0.5% OR Confidence >= 65%
  → Moderate signal, wait for market to settle

- AT_OPEN: Weak signals
  → Wait for official market open
