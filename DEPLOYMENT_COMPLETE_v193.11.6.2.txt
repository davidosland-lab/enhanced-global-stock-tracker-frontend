CRITICAL ISSUE: All three pipelines failed to start from Web Control Center

Root Cause:
  web_control_center.py was passing "--mode production" to all pipelines
  - AU pipeline script doesn't recognize --mode flag
  - UK/US pipeline scripts only accept --mode full or --mode test
  - Result: "unrecognized arguments: --mode production" error

  ✅ UK Pipeline: "--mode full" (full LSE sector scan)
