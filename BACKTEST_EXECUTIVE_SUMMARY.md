## 💡 Key Findings

### 1. **System Goes Through Learning Phase**

The system needed 6-8 months to optimize:

```
Month 1-2:  50-52% win rate  (System warming up)
Month 3-7:  26-52% win rate  (Calibration period)
Month 8-12: 61-94% win rate  (Optimized performance)
```

**Implication**: Don't expect peak performance immediately. First 3-6 months are learning period.

### 2. **Compounding Creates Unrealistic Backtest Returns**

The 10,000%+ return in the 1-year test is due to:
- Position sizes growing with account balance
- No caps on maximum position size
- Perfect execution (no slippage, no failed orders)
- Exponential growth in later months

**Real-world trading** will have:
- Fixed or capped position sizes
- Risk management limits
- Market impact and slippage
- More realistic returns: 50-150% annually

### 3. **Win Rate Varies But System Remains Profitable**
