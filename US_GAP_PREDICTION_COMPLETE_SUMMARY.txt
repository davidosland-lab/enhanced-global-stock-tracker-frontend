VIX ADJUSTMENT THRESHOLDS:
- VIX < 15: 95% confidence (very calm - slightly less predictive)
- VIX 15-25: 100% confidence (normal volatility)
- VIX 25-30: 90% confidence (elevated fear)
- VIX > 30: 80% confidence (high fear - less predictable)

WORLD RISK ADJUSTMENT:
- World Risk > 75: Reduces confidence and gap magnitude
- Factor = (world_risk - 75) / 100 (0 to 0.25)
- Confidence reduced up to 30%
- Gap dampened up to 20%
