==============================================================================
  KNOWN ISSUES & LIMITATIONS
==============================================================================

1. LSTM Training Time
   - CPU-only: 1.5-2.5 hours for 10 stocks
   - GPU: 30-50 minutes for 10 stocks
   - Mitigation: Run overnight, use GPU if available

2. Memory Requirements
   - Full system: ~2GB RAM (with all models loaded)
   - LSTM training: ~4GB RAM recommended
   - Mitigation: Close other apps during training

3. 'ta' Library Installation
   - Some platforms may have compilation issues
   - Mitigation: System works with basic indicators if 'ta' unavailable

4. News Scraping Rate Limits
   - Yahoo Finance may throttle requests
   - Mitigation: Sentiment caching (24-hour cache)

5. Accuracy Variance
   - Volatile stocks: 70-85% accuracy
   - Low-volume stocks: 65-80% accuracy
   - Mitigation: Train LSTM for specific stocks
