🎯 WHAT'S NEW IN v193.11.6.12 - CRITICAL FIX
================================================================================

✨ **SENTIMENT SCORES NOW INCLUDE GLOBAL MARKET DATA**

🔴 **Problem Fixed:**
Previous versions calculated sentiment from LOCAL data only (FTSE, VIX, GBP/USD).
Gap predictions included US/Asian/Commodity data, but sentiment didn't reflect them.

Result: Sentiment could be too optimistic when global markets were bearish.

**User Feedback Addressed:**
"The AORD was down -2.9% and the Nikkei was down -6.49%. The gap prediction 
should incorporate sentiment from the whole article and from asian markets."

✅ **Solution Implemented:**
Sentiment scores now AUTOMATICALLY ADJUSTED based on gap prediction data:

UK Pipeline:
├── Original Sentiment: 28.3/100 (local FTSE data)
├── Gap: -0.25% (includes US -1.33%, Nikkei -6.49%, AORD -2.9%)
├── Adjustment: -1.5 points (gap × 10 × 60% weight)
└── New Sentiment: 26.8/100 ✅ Reflects global conditions

US Pipeline:
├── Original Sentiment: 15.2/100 (after macro)
├── Gap: -1.04% (includes futures -2%, Nikkei -7%, Asian markets)
├── Adjustment: -7.3 points (gap × 10 × 70% weight)
└── New Sentiment: 7.9/100 ✅ Reflects global bearish conditions

================================================================================
🐛 TROUBLESHOOTING
================================================================================

ISSUE: Sentiment still seems too high/low
SOLUTION:
1. Check if gap prediction is running (look for gap_prediction in logs)
2. Verify gap_adjusted_sentiment=True in morning report
3. Check gap source data shows US/Asian percentages
4. If gap=0%, sentiment won't be adjusted (no gap data available)

ISSUE: Not seeing sentiment adjustment logs
SOLUTION:
1. Make sure you're running v193.11.6.12 (check VERSION.json)
2. Gap predictor must be enabled (ftse_predictor/spx_predictor not None)
3. Check pipeline initialization logs for predictor status

ISSUE: Gap adjustment seems too large/small
SOLUTION:
1. Formula: gap_pct × 10 × weight
2. UK weight: 60%, US weight: 70%
3. Example: -0.25% gap = -2.5 points × 60% = -1.5 point adjustment
4. Adjust weights in pipeline code if needed
