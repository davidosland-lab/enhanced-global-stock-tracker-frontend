WHY ^FTSE INDEX IS ACCEPTABLE:
==============================

1. Index tracks futures closely (arbitrage keeps spread < 0.1%)
2. For overnight predictions, previous day change is reliable
3. During trading hours, index reflects real-time market state
4. Futures only have 15% weight, so small discrepancy is negligible
5. Primary driver is US markets (35%), not futures

Impact of using index vs futures:
  - Max discrepancy: ~10 points (~0.1%)
  - Effect on prediction: 0.1% * 15% = 0.015% maximum error
  - This is well within acceptable tolerance
