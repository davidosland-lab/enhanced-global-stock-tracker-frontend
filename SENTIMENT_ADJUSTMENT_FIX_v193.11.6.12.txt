🔴 PROBLEM IDENTIFIED
================================================================================

**User Observation:**
"The sentiment scores need to be based on the whole article and a broader 
range of articles. The AORD was down -2.9% and the Nikkei was down -6.49%. 
The gap prediction should incorporate sentiment from the whole article and 
from asian markets."

**Technical Issue:**
Sentiment scores were calculated from LOCAL market data ONLY:
- UK: FTSE, VFTSE, GBP/USD
- US: S&P 500, VIX, DOW, NASDAQ

Gap predictions WERE including global data:
- US markets: S&P -1.33%, NASDAQ, DOW
- Asian markets: Nikkei -6.49%, AORD -2.9%, Hang Seng, Shanghai
- Commodities: Oil, Gold, Dollar
- Europe: DAX, CAC, STOXX

But the sentiment score DID NOT reflect these global conditions!

**Real Example from UK Pipeline:**
```
Original Sentiment: 28.3/100 ❌ Too optimistic
Gap Prediction: -0.25% (includes US -1.33%, Nikkei -6.49%, AORD -2.9%)

The gap showed global bearish conditions, but sentiment was calculated
only from local UK data and didn't incorporate this global context.
```

================================================================================
✅ SOLUTION IMPLEMENTED
================================================================================

**Approach:**
Adjust sentiment scores based on gap prediction components, which already
include US, Asian, and commodity market data.

**Formula:**
```
Gap Impact = gap_percentage × 10  # ±1% gap = ±10 sentiment points
Adjustment = Gap Impact × weight
Adjusted Sentiment = Original Sentiment + Adjustment
```

**Weights:**
- UK Pipeline: 60% weight
  (Gap includes: US 35%, Europe 30%, Futures 15%, FX 10%, Commodities 10%)

- US Pipeline: 70% weight
  (Gap includes: Futures 70%, Asian 15%, Commodities 10%, Europe 5%)

================================================================================
📊 EXAMPLE CALCULATIONS
================================================================================

UK PIPELINE (Current Market Data):
-----------------------------------
Original Sentiment: 28.3/100
  - Calculated from: FTSE, VFTSE, GBP/USD only

Gap Prediction: -0.25%
  - Includes: US -1.33%, Nikkei -6.49%, AORD -2.9%, commodities

Calculation:
  Gap Impact = -0.25% × 10 = -2.5 points
  Adjustment = -2.5 × 60% = -1.5 points
  Adjusted Sentiment = 28.3 + (-1.5) = 26.8/100 ✅

Result: Sentiment now reflects global bearish conditions!

US PIPELINE (Example):
----------------------
Original Sentiment: 15.2/100
  - After macro adjustment from macro news

Gap Prediction: -1.04%
  - Includes: Futures -2%, Nikkei -7%, Asian markets, commodities

Calculation:
  Gap Impact = -1.04% × 10 = -10.4 points
  Adjustment = -10.4 × 70% = -7.3 points
  Adjusted Sentiment = 15.2 + (-7.3) = 7.9/100 ✅

Result: Sentiment accurately reflects extreme bearish futures/Asian data!
