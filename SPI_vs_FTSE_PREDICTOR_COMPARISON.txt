PREDICTOR COMPARISON: SPI vs FTSE

COMPONENT WEIGHTS:
=================

SPI (ASX 200):
--------------
US Markets:     65%  (Primary driver - Pacific Rim correlation)
Futures:        15%  (ES=F, NQ=F - actual futures work)
UK Markets:     10%  (FTSE correlation 0.40)
Commodities:    10%  (AUD, Iron, Oil)

FTSE (UK):
----------
US Markets:     35%  (Overnight influence, time zone lag)
Europe:         30%  (Same-day close, strong correlation)
Futures:        15%  (^FTSE index as proxy - Z=F doesn't work)
FX:             10%  (GBP/USD, EUR/GBP)
Commodities:    10%  (Oil, Gold, Copper)

ANALYSIS:
========
✓ Different weightings make sense:
  - ASX more dependent on US (65%) due to Pacific Rim
  - FTSE more dependent on Europe (30%) due to geographic proximity
  - FTSE has FX component (10%), SPI doesn't (FX embedded in commodities)
