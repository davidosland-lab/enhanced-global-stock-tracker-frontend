Trade Mode Options:
-------------------
1. "strict" (default, conservative)
   - Only BUY/STRONG_BUY signals are traded
   - HOLD signals are always blocked
   - Recommended for live trading

2. "confidence_based" (aggressive)
   - Confidence ≥53% is primary filter
   - HOLD with score ≥60 → treated as BUY
   - HOLD with score ≤40 → can trigger SELL
   - HOLD with score 40-60 → blocked
   - Recommended for paper trading/testing

Change via Dashboard:
---------------------
1. Open http://localhost:8050
2. Navigate to "Trading Controls"
3. Select Trade Mode from dropdown
4. Changes apply immediately

 PERFORMANCE TIPS

   - Start with 53% (balanced)
