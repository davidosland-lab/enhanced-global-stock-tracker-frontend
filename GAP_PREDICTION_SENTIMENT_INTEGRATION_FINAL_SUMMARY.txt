✅ SOLUTION IMPLEMENTED
================================================================================

**NEW: Gap Prediction Data Now Adjusts Sentiment Score**

Formula:
────────
Gap Impact = gap_percentage × 10  # ±1% gap = ±10 sentiment points
Adjustment = Gap Impact × weight
Adjusted Sentiment = Original Sentiment + Adjustment

Weights:
────────
UK Pipeline: 60% weight
  (Gap includes: US 35%, Europe 30%, Futures 15%, FX 10%, Commodities 10%)

US Pipeline: 70% weight
  (Gap includes: Futures 70%, Asian 15%, Commodities 10%, Europe 5%)

**REAL EXAMPLE - UK Pipeline (Your Data):**
────────────────────────────────────────────
Original Sentiment: 28.3/100
  - Calculated from: FTSE, VFTSE, GBP/USD only

Gap Prediction: -0.25%
  - Includes: US -1.33%, Nikkei -6.49%, AORD -2.9%, commodities

Calculation:
  Gap Impact = -0.25% × 10 = -2.5 points
  Adjustment = -2.5 × 60% = -1.5 points
  Adjusted Sentiment = 28.3 + (-1.5) = 26.8/100 ✅

Result: Sentiment NOW reflects global bearish conditions!
