================================================================================
                          HOW IT WORKS (SIMPLE)
================================================================================

Step 1: Fetch Data
├─ Price history (60 days for LSTM)
├─ News articles (10-20 recent)
└─ Volume data (20 days)

Step 2: Run 5 Models in Parallel
├─ LSTM analyzes patterns
├─ Trend calculates momentum
├─ Technical runs 8 indicators
├─ Sentiment analyzes news
└─ Volume checks conviction

Step 3: Combine with Weights
├─ Calculate weighted price
├─ Calculate weighted confidence
├─ Vote for direction (BUY/SELL/HOLD)
└─ Apply volume adjustment

Step 4: Return Final Prediction
├─ Signal: BUY/SELL/HOLD
├─ Target price
├─ Confidence: 50-95%
└─ Full breakdown
