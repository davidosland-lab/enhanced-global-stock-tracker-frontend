===============================================================================
                        TROUBLESHOOTING
===============================================================================

  PROBLEM: ngrok not found
  SOLUTION: Install from https://ngrok.com/download

  PROBLEM: Can't connect from mobile
  SOLUTION: Check dashboard running at http://localhost:8050
           Try incognito/private mode

  PROBLEM: Wrong credentials
  SOLUTION: Check config/mobile_connection_info.txt

  PROBLEM: Tunnel expired
  SOLUTION: Free tier expires after 2 hours inactive
           Just restart START_MOBILE_ACCESS.bat

  PROBLEM: Slow on mobile
  SOLUTION: Use WiFi, close other apps, clear browser cache

  Full troubleshooting: See QUICK_START_MOBILE.md
