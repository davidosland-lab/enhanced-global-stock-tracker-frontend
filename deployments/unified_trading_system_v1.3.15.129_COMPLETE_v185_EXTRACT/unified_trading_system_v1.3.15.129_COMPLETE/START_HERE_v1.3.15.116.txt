 WHAT'S FIXED IN v1.3.15.116
================================================================================

Fix 1: 24hr Market Chart Not Updating (CRITICAL)
  Problem: Chart showed yesterday's data, never updated
  Cause:   Used single date filter instead of 24-hour rolling window
  Fix:     Changed to show last 24 hours continuously
  Impact:  Chart now updates every 5 minutes with live data

  Before: Market hours data: 26 points, Date filter: 2026-02-10
  After:  24h window data: 96, Date range: 2026-02-10 to 2026-02-11

  Problem: Reports saved to pipelines\reports\ (hidden location)
  Cause:   Path calculation one level too shallow
  Fix:     Added one more .parent to reach project root
  Impact:  Reports now in reports\morning_reports\ (correct location)

  Before: pipelines\reports\morning_reports\*.html
  After:  reports\morning_reports\*.html
