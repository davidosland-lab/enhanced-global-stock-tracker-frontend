Original Formula:
```python
world_risk_score = (
    neg_sent * 30 +      # Negative sentiment: 30% weight
    fear * 35 +          # Fear emotion: 35% weight  
    anger * 20 +         # Anger emotion: 20% weight
    topic_severity * 15  # Topic severity: only 15% weight! ❌

```

PROBLEM:
For a major war (severity = 95):
- Topic contributes: 0.95 * 15 = 14.25 points (only 14%!)

- Anger contributes: 0.00 * 20 = 0 points

- **TOTAL: 20.2 points out of 100!** ❌

Result: Major war (severity 95) → shows 16-20/100 instead of 85-90/100!

WHY THIS IS WRONG:
- Topic severity is the PRIMARY indicator of crisis
- major_war (95), military_conflict (85), nuclear_threat (100)
- These should DOMINATE the score, not contribute just 15%!
- Emotions (fear/anger) should be SECONDARY boosts
- Negative sentiment should be TERTIARY confirmation

NEW FORMULA:
━━━━━━━━━━━

```python
# v193.4 FIX: Topic severity should dominate the score
# Start with topic severity as base (60-95% of score)
base_risk = topic_severity * 100  # Convert 0-1 back to 0-100

# Add emotional intensity boost (can add up to 35 points)
emotion_boost = (fear * 20) + (anger * 15)

# Add negative sentiment boost (can add up to 20 points)
sentiment_boost = neg_sent * 20

# Total risk score
world_risk_score = base_risk + emotion_boost + sentiment_boost

# Clamp to 0-100
world_risk_score = max(0, min(100, world_risk_score))
```

LOGIC:
━━━━━━

1. **Topic Severity = Primary Driver**
   - major_war (95) → base_risk = 95 points
   - military_conflict (85) → base_risk = 85 points
   - nuclear_threat (100) → base_risk = 100 points
   - This is the FOUNDATION of the risk score

2. **Emotions = Secondary Boost**
   - Fear (0.0-1.0) can add 0-20 points
   - Anger (0.0-1.0) can add 0-15 points
   - Total emotional boost: 0-35 points
   - Amplifies risk when headlines show fear/anger

3. **Negative Sentiment = Tertiary Confirmation**
   - Neg_sent (0.0-1.0) can add 0-20 points
   - FinBERT-detected negativity confirms crisis
   - Less important than topics and emotions

WHY THIS HAPPENED:
━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━

Original formula designer gave equal-ish weights to all components:
- neg_sent: 30%
- fear: 35%
- anger: 20%
- topic_severity: 15%

This assumes emotional signals are MORE important than topic severity.

WRONG ASSUMPTION:
Topic severity is the GROUND TRUTH:
- "major_war" in title = 95% certain it's a major war
- "nuclear_threat" in title = 100% certain it's nuclear
- "oil_shock" in title = 75% certain it's oil crisis

Emotions are SECONDARY indicators:
- Fear keywords may or may not be present
- Anger keywords may or may not be present
- Sentiment may be neutral (reporting facts)

The fix makes topic severity PRIMARY (60-100% of score)
and emotions/sentiment SECONDARY (0-40% boost).
