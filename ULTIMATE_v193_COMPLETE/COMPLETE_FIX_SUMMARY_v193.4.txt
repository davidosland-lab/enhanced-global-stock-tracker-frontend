🎯 TECHNICAL SUMMARY
═══════════════════════════════════════════════════════════════════════

PROBLEM:
World Event Risk Monitor algorithm was detecting crisis topics correctly
but risk scoring formula gave topic severity way too little weight (15%).

WHY THIS HAPPENED:
Original formula designer assumed emotions (fear, anger) and sentiment
were more important than topic detection. This is backwards:
- Topic severity = Ground Truth (90-95% accurate)
- Emotions = Secondary indicators (may or may not be present)
- Sentiment = Tertiary confirmation (often neutral for factual reporting)

SOLUTION:
Inverted the weight hierarchy:
- Topic Severity: 60-100% (primary driver)
- Emotions: 0-35 points (secondary boost)
- Sentiment: 0-20 points (tertiary confirmation)

RESULT:
System now correctly detects and responds to geopolitical crises,
automatically protecting positions during extreme market events.
