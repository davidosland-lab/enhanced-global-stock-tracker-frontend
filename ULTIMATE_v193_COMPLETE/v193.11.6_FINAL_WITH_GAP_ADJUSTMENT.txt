🧮 GAP ADJUSTMENT FORMULA
================================================================================

**Step 1: Calculate Sentiment Deviation**
```
sentiment_deviation = (final_sentiment - 50) / 50
```
Range: -1.0 (extremely bearish) to +1.0 (extremely bullish)

**Step 2: Adjust Gap**
```
adjusted_gap = original_gap * (1 + sentiment_deviation * 0.5)
```

**Examples:**

Scenario 1: War Risk (Your Current Situation)
  Original Gap: +0.11%
  Sentiment: 46.3 → 41.9 (macro) → 26.1 (world risk)
  Deviation: (26.1 - 50) / 50 = -0.478
  Adjusted Gap: +0.11% * (1 + (-0.478 * 0.5))
              = +0.11% * (1 - 0.239)
              = +0.11% * 0.761
              = +0.08%
  Impact: Gap reduced by 48% due to extreme world risk

Scenario 2: Bullish News
  Original Gap: +0.20%
  Sentiment: 46 → 55 (macro) → 68 (low risk)
  Deviation: (68 - 50) / 50 = +0.36
  Adjusted Gap: +0.20% * (1 + (0.36 * 0.5))
              = +0.20% * 1.18
              = +0.24%
  Impact: Gap increased by 18% due to positive sentiment

Scenario 3: Bearish Gap + War Risk
  Original Gap: -0.50%
  Sentiment: 45 → 35 (macro) → 22 (extreme risk)
  Deviation: (22 - 50) / 50 = -0.56
  Adjusted Gap: -0.50% * (1 + (-0.56 * 0.5))
              = -0.50% * 0.72
              = -0.36%
  Impact: Negative gap reduced (less bearish) by 28%
