PROBLEM SUMMARY
================================================================================

World Event Risk Monitor consistently returns 50/100 (MODERATE) instead of
85-90/100 (EXTREME) for Iran-Israel-US conflict because it's NOT receiving
the critical geopolitical articles.

USER LOG SHOWS:
```

  Topics:  [EMPTY!]
  
Recent World Headlines:
  1. RBA Speech: 2026
  2. RBA Speech: 2025
  3. RBA Speech: 2024
```

EXPECTED OUTPUT:
```
[OK] Passing 11 articles for world risk analysis
[World Event Risk] Score: 85.4/100 (EXTREME)
  Base Risk (topics): 85.0, Emotion Boost: 8.5, Sentiment Boost: 2.9
  Topics: military_conflict, major_war
  
Recent World Headlines:
  1. Trump says 'everything's been knocked out in Iran'...
  2. Gas and oil prices soar on fears conflict spreads...
  3. US nationals urged to leave Middle East...
```

================================================================================
ROOT CAUSE
================================================================================

FILE: pipelines/models/screening/macro_news_monitor.py
LINES: 415, 458, 500 (US, ASX, UK pipelines)

The macro news monitor was limiting articles to the first 5:

```python
result = {
    'market': 'ASX',
    'article_count': len(articles),  # Reports 11 articles collected
    'articles': articles[:5],  # But only passes first 5 to World Risk!
    'top_articles': articles[:5],
    ...
}
```

ARTICLE ORDER PROBLEM:
1. Central bank articles added first (RBA/Fed/BoE speeches: positions 0-2)
2. Global geopolitical articles added second (wars, crises: positions 3-10)

When taking articles[:5], you get:
- Articles[0:2] = 3 central bank speeches
- Articles[3:4] = 2 global articles (NOT ENOUGH!)
- Articles[5:10] = 6 critical war articles (EXCLUDED!)

World Event Monitor needs ALL articles to detect:
- Topic keywords: "war", "Iran", "Israel", "military", "strike"
- Emotion keywords: "fear", "panic", "attack", "threat"
- Negative sentiment from full article text

With only 2 out of 8 war articles, topic detection FAILS:
- Not enough keyword matches to trigger "military_conflict" (threshold ~3 articles)
- Not enough keyword matches to trigger "major_war" (threshold ~2 articles)
- Emotion keywords diluted by neutral central bank speech text
- Base risk defaults to 50/100 (neutral baseline)

================================================================================
WHY THIS BUG WAS HARD TO FIND
================================================================================

1. **Misleading Log Output:**
   - Log said "11 articles collected" ✓
   - But only 5 were actually passed ✗
   - article_count vs len(articles) discrepancy

2. **v193.4 Formula Was Correct:**
   - Formula fix applied correctly ✓
   - But had no articles to analyze ✗
   - "Garbage in, garbage out"

3. **Silent Truncation:**
   - articles[:5] doesn't error or warn
   - Just silently drops critical data
   - No indication that 6 articles were lost

4. **Order-Dependent Failure:**
   - If war articles were collected first (positions 0-4), it would work
   - But central bank articles are fetched first (positions 0-2)
   - War articles end up at positions 5-10 and get dropped

5. **Testing Gap:**
   - Testing focused on formula correctness (v193.4) ✓
   - Didn't verify full article list was passed ✗
   - Assumed articles field contained all collected articles

================================================================================
LESSONS LEARNED
================================================================================

1. **Don't Truncate Input Data:**
   - Only limit data for DISPLAY purposes
   - Pass full datasets to ANALYSIS functions
   - Separate display_articles from analysis_articles

2. **Verify Data Flow End-to-End:**
   - Check not just formula, but inputs
   - Log article count at collection AND analysis
   - Compare "collected" vs "analyzed" counts

3. **Prioritize Critical Articles:**
   - Consider sorting by importance before truncation
   - Put geopolitical events FIRST, central bank speeches LAST
   - Or better: don't truncate analysis data at all

4. **Add Validation Warnings:**
   - Warn if World Risk gets < 5 articles
   - Warn if no topics detected despite having articles
   - Log article titles being analyzed (not just count)
