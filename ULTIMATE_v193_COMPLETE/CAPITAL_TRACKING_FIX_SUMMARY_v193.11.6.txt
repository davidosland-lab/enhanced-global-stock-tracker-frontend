================================================================================
CAPITAL CALCULATION FORMULA (VERIFIED CORRECT)
================================================================================

The PaperTradingCoordinator uses the correct industry-standard formula:

    Total Capital = Available Cash + Current Position Values

Where:
- Available Cash = self.current_capital (updated after each trade)
- Position Values = Σ(shares × current_price) for all open positions

Example:
- Starting capital: $100,000
- Buy 100 shares @ $500 = $50,000 cost
- After buy: Cash = $50,000, Positions = $50,000, Total = $100,000 ✓
- Price rises to $510
- Now: Cash = $50,000, Positions = $51,000, Total = $101,000 ✓
