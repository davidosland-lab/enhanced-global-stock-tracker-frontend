================================================================================
ROOT CAUSE
================================================================================

Emotion detection analyzed only article TITLES:
- Titles are short (~10-15 words)
- Titles focus on facts, not emotions
- Example: "Gas and oil prices soar and shares tumble on fears conflict..."
  * Only emotion keyword: "fears" (1 word out of 12)
  * Fear score: 1/12 = 0.08 (too low)

Full article text contains many more emotion keywords:
- Articles are ~200-500 words
- Body text includes: "panic," "threat," "escalation," "attack," "concern"
- Example full article might have 15-20 emotion keywords
- Fear score: 15/400 = 0.375 (realistic)
