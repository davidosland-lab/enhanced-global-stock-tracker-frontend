QUESTION 3 (BUG FIX): REGIME DETECTOR FALSE RALLY - CRITICAL FIX APPLIED
================================================================================

**USER OBSERVATION**: 
"AU pipeline log shows S&P -0.6%, NASDAQ -0.3%, VIX 23.8 but regime says 
US_BROAD_RALLY with explanation 'Broad US market rally. ASX may see modest gains'"

**ROOT CAUSE**: 🚨 **CRITICAL BUG IN market_regime_detector.py LINE 360**

THE BUG:
--------
```python
# Comment says "Default to neutral"
return MarketRegime.US_BROAD_RALLY, 0.3  # But code returns RALLY!
```

When regime detector couldn't determine market regime from data (empty scores dict),
it would fall back to US_BROAD_RALLY - creating FALSE BULLISH SIGNALS in down markets.

THE FIX:
--------
```python
# Default to risk-off when unable to determine regime (conservative approach)
logger.warning("[REGIME] Unable to determine regime from market data, defaulting to US_RISK_OFF")
return MarketRegime.US_RISK_OFF, 0.3
```

RATIONALE:
----------
✅ Conservative approach: When uncertain, be defensive (protect capital)
✅ US_RISK_OFF is appropriate for equity trading during uncertainty
✅ Matches risk management philosophy: "when in doubt, reduce risk"
✅ Added warning log for operator visibility
✅ No breaking changes (same return signature)

IMPACT OF BUG (BEFORE FIX):
---------------------------
❌ False bullish signals during down markets
❌ Incorrect sector allocations:
   - Materials: Favored (+0.30) when should be avoided (-0.40)
   - Financials: OK (+0.20) when should be cautious (-0.30)
   - Energy: OK (+0.10) when should be cautious (-0.30)
❌ Aggressive position sizing (1.0x) when should be defensive (0.5-0.7x)
❌ Risk management gates may not trigger appropriately
❌ Misleading logs confuse operators
❌ Potential trading losses from bullish bias in bearish conditions

IMPACT OF FIX (AFTER):
----------------------
✅ Accurate regime detection in uncertain markets
✅ Conservative sector allocations:
   - Defensives favored: Healthcare +0.30, Consumer Staples +0.20
   - Cyclicals avoided: Materials -0.40, Financials -0.30
✅ Appropriate position sizing (reduced in uncertainty)
✅ Risk gates trigger correctly
✅ Clear warning logs when regime uncertain
✅ Protects capital during volatility
